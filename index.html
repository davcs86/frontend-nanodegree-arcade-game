Rules
  • Move your character with right, left, up and down keys.
  • You start with 2:00 as time limit to get as many points as you can:
    • You get +200 of score when you reach the water area.
    • You get -50 of score and -0:10 of time when you collide with a bug.
    • and you get more time by collecting the gems (Blue: +0:30, Green: +0:20, Orange: +0:10). But, collect them as fast as possible since they disappear after a while.
  • Have fun!
